# Sets 
$m \in model$ 
& Set of \href{https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept_reference/Object\%20Classes/#model}{model} objects that specifies general parameters. \\ 

$n \in node$ 
& Set of [node](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#node) objects in the model \\ 

$u \in unit$ 
& Set of [unit](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#unit) objects in the model \\ 

$uc \in unit\_constraint$ 
& Set of [unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#unit\_constraint) objects in the model \\ 

$conn \in connection$ 
& Set of [connection](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#connection) objects in the model \\ 

$c \in commodity$ 
& Set of \href{https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept_reference/Object%20Classes/#commodity}{commodity} objects in the model. \\ 

$d \in direction$ 
& Set of flow direction associated with a node, including ``to\_node`` and ``from\_node`` \\ 

$s \in stochastic\_scenario$ 
& Set of [stochastic\_scenario](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#stochastic\_scenario) objects in the model \\ 

$s \in stochastic\_path$ 
& Set of unique sequences of stochastic scenarios that traverse the stochastic direct acyclic graph (DAG) from root to leaves \\ 

$tb \in temporal\_block$ 
& Set of [temporal\_block](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Object%20Classes/#temporal\_block) objects in the model \\ 

$ng \in groups(node=n)$ 
& Set of node objects that belong to the same [node\_group](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/the\_basics/#Introduction-to-groups-of-objects) \\ 

$.. \in ind(*parameter*)$ 
& Tuple of all objects, for which the parameter is defined \\ 

$i \in N$ 
& Set of indexes for navigating an array value \\ 

$t \in TimeSlice$ 
& Set of specific time-indexes for navigating a time-varying value \\ 

$t \in t\_before\_t(t\_after=t')$ 
& Set of timeslices that are directly before timeslice t' \\ 

$t \in t\_before\_t(t\_before=t')$ 
& Set of timeslices that are directly after timeslice t' \\ 

$t \in t\_in\_t(t\_short=t')$ 
& Set of timeslices that contain timeslice t' \\ 

$t \in t\_in\_t(t\_long=t')$ 
& Set of timeslices that are contained in timeslice t' \\ 

$t \in t\_overlaps\_t(t')$ 
& Set of timeslices that overlap with timeslice t' \\ 

$t \in t\_lowest\_resolution(node=n, temporal\_block=tb)$ 
&  Set of the lowest resolution timeslices in node n and temporal block tb \\ 

$[s\_path] \in full\_stochastic\_paths$ 
& Set of all possible sequences of stochastic scenarios that traverse the stochastic direct acyclic graph (DAG) from root to leaves \\ 

$[s\_path] \in active\_stochastic\_paths(s)$ 
& Set of sequences of stochastic scenarios that contain the active scenarios s \\ 

$(n, tb) \in node\_\_temporal\_block$ 
& Set of tuples of node and temporal block being defined under the relationship [node\_\_temporal\_block](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#node\_\_temporal\_block) \\ 

$(u, n) \in unit\_\_node$ 
& Set of tuples of unit and node being defined under the relationship [unit\_\_to\_node](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#unit\_\_to\_node) or [unit\_\_from\_node](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#unit\_\_from\_node) \\ 

$(u, n, uc) \in unit\_\_node\_\_unit\_constraint$ 
& Set of tuples of unit, node and unit constraint being defined under the relationship [unit\_\_to\_node\_\_unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#unit\_\_to\_node\_\_unit\_constraint) or [unit\_\_from\_node\_\_unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#unit\_\_from\_node\_\_unit\_constraint) \\ 

$(conn, n, uc) \in connection\_\_node\_\_unit\_constraint$ 
& Set of tuples of connection, node and unit constraint being defined under the relationship [connection\_\_to\_node\_\_unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#connection\_\_to\_node\_\_unit\_constraint) or [connection\_\_from\_node\_\_unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#connection\_\_from\_node\_\_unit\_constraint) \\ 

$(u, uc) \in unit\_\_unit\_constraint$ 
& Set of tuples of unit and unit constraint being defined under the relationship [unit\_\_unit\_constraint](https://spine-project.github.io/SpineOpt.jl/latest/concept\_reference/Relationship%20Classes/#unit\_\_unit\_constraint) \\
